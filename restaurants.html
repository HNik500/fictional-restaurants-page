ABOUT US

Welcome to Nikhil Restaurant, where culinary diversity meets exquisite taste. Embark on a flavorful journey spanning continents as we invite you to indulge in a vibrant fusion of Indian, West African, and Middle Eastern cuisines. Our expert chefs blend traditional recipes with contemporary flair, crafting a menu that tantalizes the taste buds and ignites the senses. From aromatic spices to rich sauces, each dish tells a story of cultural heritage and culinary mastery. Whether you crave the bold flavors of curry, the savory delights of jollof rice, or the aromatic essence of falafel, Nikhil Restaurant promises a culinary adventure like no other. Join us and savor the world on a plate at Nikhil Restaurant.

OUR MENU

Title: Flavorful Biriyani Delight

Indulge in the aromatic blend of bas mati rice, tender meat or vegetables, and a symphony of spices in our signature biryani. Each bite is a journey through layers of rich flavors and textures, perfectly balanced to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you crave the succulence of chicken, the tenderness of lamb, or the freshness of vegetables, our biryani promises a culinary experience like no other.

Price: $12.99 per serving

Jollof Rice Fiesta

Dive into a culinary adventure with our vibrant Jollof Rice, a beloved West African classic bursting with flavor. Savor the perfect harmony of long-grain rice simmered in a tantalizing tomato-based sauce, infused with aromatic spices and enriched with succulent pieces of chicken or vegetables. Each spoonful is a celebration of tradition and taste, guaranteed to transport you to the heart of Africa with its bold and unforgettable flavors.

$10.99 per serving

Doner Kebab

Seasoned meat stacked in a cone shape, roasted on a vertical spit, and shaved off in thin slices. Typically served in a pita bread wrap with salad, onions, tomatoes, and a yogurt or tahini sauce. Embark on a journey of taste with our succulent Kebab platter, where juicy marinated meat or flavorful vegetables are skillfully grilled to perfection. Each tender bite is a symphony of spices and textures, combining smoky char with delicate tenderness. Served alongside fragrant rice, crisp salad, and creamy sauces, our Kebab platter is a culinary delight that promises to satisfy every craving and leave you longing for more.

$15.99 per serving
